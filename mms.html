Magic Morning Smoothie

Ingredients

Make it yourself with the below ingredients

  • ½ cup organic blueberries
  • ½ cup vanilla almond milk
  • ¼ cup yogurt
  • 1 banana
  • 1 leaf/stem kale
  • 1 scoop of Vanilla Whey Protein
  • 1 teaspoon Ashwagandha

How to Prepare

  1. Blend all ingredients for 20-30seconds
  2. You can add some ice with you want to
